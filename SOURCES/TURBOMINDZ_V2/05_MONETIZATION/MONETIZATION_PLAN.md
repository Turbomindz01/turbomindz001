## PART 1: CROWDFUNDING STRATEGY (NFT-AS-CROWDFUNDING MODEL)

### 1.1 THE CONCEPT: NFT COLLECTION AS CROWDFUNDING

Traditional crowdfunding asks supporters to fund a project with no direct ownership. TURBOMINDZ inverts this: **supporters buy NFTs, which directly fund production while giving them verifiable ownership and community status.**

**Why This Works:**
- Lower barrier: NFTs are digital, no shipping required
- Verifiable scarcity: 1,111 hard cap, transparent blockchain
- Perpetual supporter value: Royalties, perks, and future utility
- Web3-native: Attracts crypto-savvy early adopters
- Transparent funding: Every mint and royalty is visible on-chain
