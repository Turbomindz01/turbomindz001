## 12 PINTEREST-SPECIFIC IMAGE PROMPTS (V2)

All images generated in **Nano Banana 2** at **2:3 vertical** aspect ratio, **2K or 4K** resolution.

### Design Principles (What Changed from V1)
1. **Outsider-first appeal** — images must stop the scroll of someone who has never heard of Turbomindz. Only 2 of 12 feature named characters; the rest are universally stunning standalone art.
2. **Color palette variety** — each pin uses a distinct palette to show the range of 39 universes (cool blues, emerald greens, warm ambers, deep purples, sunset oranges, midnight indigos) instead of defaulting to gold-and-teal.
3. **Composition variety** — mix of wide establishing shots, extreme close-ups, overhead flat-lays, macro detail crops, and eye-level intimate scenes. No two compositions use the same camera angle.
4. **Search-intent alignment** — each prompt targets what real Pinterest users actually search for ("cozy aesthetic," "art inspiration," "satisfying miniature," "philosophical art print," "cute character design").
5. **Save-worthy quality** — every image should make someone think "I want that on my wall" or "I need to come back to this." Wallpaper-grade, reference-grade, or mood-board-grade.
6. **Even board coverage** — every board gets at least 1 pin, no board gets more than 3.
7. **No text reliance** — no prompts require AI to render readable words (AI mangles text). Inscriptions are atmosphere, not focal point.
8. **Varied style anchors** — the claymation/Aardman core is preserved but described differently each time to produce diverse outputs.

## PIN CREATION CHECKLIST

For each pin:

1. **Image:** 2:3 vertical (1000x1500px) — generate in Nano Banana 2 with aspect ratio set to 2:3
2. **Title (100 chars max):** Keyword-rich, descriptive — NOT a branded tagline
   - Good: "Claymation Astronaut in Renaissance Alchemical Cathedral | Stop Motion Art"
   - Bad: "Turbomindz Scene 1 — The First Question"
3. **Description (500 chars max):** Keyword-rich paragraph. Include: what's in the image, art style, mood, Turbomindz mention, easter eggs callout
4. **Alt Text:** Literal description of the image for accessibility
5. **Destination Link:** linktr.ee/turbomindz (default) or direct OpenSea link for specific NFTs
6. **Board:** Select primary board, then save to 1-2 secondary boards
7. **Publish**
