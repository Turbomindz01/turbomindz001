## 1. PROJECT IDENTITY

**Turbomindz** is a philosophical art universe that lives simultaneously as an NFT collection, an episodic animated series, a community, and a Progressive Web App.

**Tagline:** *Create. Connect. Trade Fair.*

**The Elevator Pitch:** Four claymation characters race through 39 dying universes — each one born from a question humanity once asked — creating art to keep them alive. Every NFT is a frame from the film. Every collector is a witness. Every piece of physical art married to an NFT proves that digital and physical were never enemies.

**Collection Size:** 1,111 NFTs across 39 episodes
**Production Rate:** 4 NFTs per day (~278 production days)
**Episode Count:** 39 episodes (one per universe), structured in 3 seasons
**Film Format:** Episodic animated series, 2-5 minutes per episode
**Blockchain:** Polygon (ERC-721)
**Budget Ceiling:** Under $50/month for tools and services

## 2. THE SOUL OF TURBOMINDZ

Everything in this project flows from five principles. If a decision doesn't serve at least one of these, it doesn't belong here.

**Philosophy First** — We lead with questions, not products. Every NFT contains a public domain quote from a thinker who died before 1956. Every scene explores one of 9 philosophical themes. We sell wonder, not speculation.

**Art is Sacred** — The claymation aesthetic isn't a gimmick. It represents craft, imperfection, and the human hand. Pixar meets Aardman. Warm, tactile, luminous. Every image earns its existence through beauty and meaning.

**Community is the Village** — Collectors aren't customers. They're travelers, witnesses, villagers. The Discord isn't a chat server — it's the village square. The marketplace isn't a store — it's a bazaar where stories are exchanged.

**Transparency is Trust** — All trades are public (Witnessed Economy). All governance is visible. The founder's face is never shown — the four characters are the public identity. The project builds in public.

**Digital and Physical are One** — The marriage system permanently binds NFTs to physical artworks. This isn't a metaphor. The NFT is the soul. The art is the body. Together they're complete. Apart they're just data and canvas.

## 7. THE MARRIAGE SYSTEM (Self-Governing)

The marriage system permanently binds an NFT (digital soul) to a physical artwork (body). It is **self-service by design** — the community governs itself.

### How It Works
1. Own a Turbomindz NFT
2. Create or acquire a physical artwork
3. Photograph the artwork from 6+ angles (Front, Back, Top, Bottom, Left, Right + 2 optional detail shots)
4. Submit a marriage through the app — upload photos, enter artwork details (title, medium, dimensions)
5. **Marriage is recorded automatically on-chain (Polygon)** — NO founder approval needed
6. NFT metadata updates: "Marriage Status: Married"
7. The NFT and artwork become a permanent pair — phygital art

### Self-Governing Rules
- **First marriage = instant and free.** No approval. No waiting. Upload photos, confirm, done.
- **Changing the married artwork (divorce + remarriage) = requires community review.** This prevents fraud. When a collector wants to swap the physical art linked to their NFT, the request goes to a community vote by @Elder and @Scribe role holders. 3 of 5 votes approve the change.
- **Re-marriage fee: $20 MATIC** — covers the on-chain update and community review time
- **Community flagging:** Any member can flag a suspicious marriage (e.g., stock photos, AI-generated fakes). Flagged marriages go to community review. 3 flags from unique members triggers automatic review.
- **The Provenance Museum:** All marriages and their photo evidence are permanently public — transparency IS the verification.

### Why Marriage Matters
- Only married NFTs can be listed on the Turbomindz marketplace
- Married NFTs trade as phygital art (both digital + physical transfer)
- The 2.5% marketplace commission applies to phygital trades
- Self-service marriage keeps the system running without founder bottlenecks
- Community governance makes the project resilient and decentralized
