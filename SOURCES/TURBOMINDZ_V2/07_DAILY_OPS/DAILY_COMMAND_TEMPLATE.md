# TURBOMINDZ DAILY COMMAND TEMPLATE
## "Today's Tasks" — The Command You Run Every Day

---

## HOW THIS WORKS

Every day, you say: **"Today's tasks"**

Claude will then generate the following package for that day's 4 NFTs + film production + social media, based on where you are in the 39-episode, 1,111-scene odyssey.

---

## DAILY OUTPUT PACKAGE

### For Each of the 4 NFTs:

**1. Scene Card**
- NFT ID (e.g., TM-029)
- Title
- Episode number and name
- Universe + Art Tradition
- Theme + Core Question
- Characters present
- Scene position in episode (e.g., Shot 3 of 28: "THE WONDER")

**2. Nano Banana 2 Image Prompt**
- Full descriptive prose prompt (no flags)
- Recommended aspect ratio: 4:5
- Recommended resolution: 2K or 4K
- Style anchors included (claymation, Pixar meets Aardman, etc.)

**3. NFT Description**
- Scene number and act
- 3-4 sentence narrative description (in Turbomindz voice)
- Public domain quote + author (died before 1956)
- "This scene contains 5 hidden easter eggs" callout
- Turbomindz tagline

**4. Five Easter Eggs**
- At least 1 foreshadowing a future scene
- At least 1 referencing the current universe's art tradition
- At least 1 tiny hidden character/object
- 2 creative freedom

**5. OpenSea Metadata**
- Scene ID, Act, Chapter, Universe, Theme, Characters
- Quote Author, Price Tier, Easter Eggs count, Marriage Status
- Exact price in MATIC

**6. Higgsfield.ai Video Prompt**
- Image-to-video prompt for animating the NFT image
- Camera movement direction (pan, zoom, orbit, static)
- Duration: 10-15 seconds
- Mood/pacing notes
- Model recommendation (WAN 2.5 for continuity, Veo 3.1 for quality)

### Social Media Content (Daily):

**7. Instagram Caption**
- Scene reveal text (philosophical, not salesy)
- PD quote
- 5 relevant hashtags
- CTA: "Link in bio to explore the collection"

**8. Twitter/X Post**
- Abbreviated version (280 chars)
- Include scene image
- Hashtags: #Turbomindz #NFTArt #PhilosophicalArt

**9. Discord Announcement**
- Embed format for #announcements channel
- Scene title, quote, easter egg hint, OpenSea link

**10. Pinterest Daily Pin**
- Select best scene of the day for Pinterest vertical crop
- **Pin Image:** 2:3 vertical (1000x1500px) — crop from the 4:5 NFT image in Nano Banana 2
- **Pin Title:** Keyword-rich, descriptive (100 chars max) — NOT a branded tagline
  - Example: "Claymation Astronaut in Renaissance Cathedral | Philosophical Stop Motion Art"
- **Pin Description:** 300-500 characters, keyword-rich paragraph
  - Include: what's in the image, art style, mood, Turbomindz mention, collection context
  - Weave in natural keyword phrases (claymation art, philosophical art, stop motion animation, NFT collectible, hidden easter eggs)
- **Board Assignment:** Pin to primary board + save to 1-2 secondary boards
  - Rotate across all 10 boards to keep them active and dense
- **Destination Link:** Linktree URL or direct OpenSea link for that NFT
- **Alt Text:** Literal image description for accessibility
- **Curated Repins (2-3 per day, ~2 minutes):**
  - Repin 2-3 relevant pins from other creators to your boards
  - Sources: claymation artists, philosophy quote pages, miniature/diorama art, space art, cozy aesthetic
  - Keeps boards growing and signals active curation to Pinterest algorithm

**11. Episode Progress Update**
- "Episode X: Y of 28 scenes complete"
- "Z days until episode assembly"

## WEEKLY OUTPUT (Every 7th Day — Episode Assembly Day)

In addition to daily tasks, every 7th day includes:

**11. Episode Assembly Checklist**
- Stitch 28 video clips in DaVinci Resolve
- Add transitions, color grading, audio
- Export at 1080p
- Upload to YouTube with metadata
- Create episode thumbnail

**12. Newsletter Draft**
- "The Odyssey Dispatch" — Weekly newsletter
- Written in Cosmo's voice
- Sections: Personal opening, this week's scenes, Nova's Pattern, featured product, community highlight, easter egg hint

**13. Patreon Update**
- Behind-the-scenes of the week's production
- Preview of next episode's universe

**14. Pinterest Weekly Review & Idea Pin**
- Verify all 7 daily pins were posted (one per day's best scene)
- Check board health: each board should have 20+ total pins (mix of original + curated)
- Create 1 **Idea Pin** (multi-slide, highest engagement format):
  - Suggested formats:
    - "5 Hidden Easter Eggs in This Week's Scenes" (one reveal per slide)
    - "How We Make Philosophical Claymation Art" (3-5 step process)
    - "Which Philosophy Quote Speaks to You?" (carousel of quotes with scenes)
    - "The 4 Characters of Turbomindz" (one character per slide)
    - "This Week in the Odyssey" (7 scenes, one per slide, story recap)
  - Idea Pins get 9x more engagement than standard pins — prioritize these weekly
- Review Pinterest Analytics: top-performing pins, audience demographics, trending keywords
- Adjust board rotation and keyword strategy based on what's getting traction

**15. Next Episode Planning**
- Shot list for next 28 scenes
- Quote selection (7 unique PD quotes)
- Easter egg master plan
- Character development beats
