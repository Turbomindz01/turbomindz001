## REVISED MARRIAGE SYSTEM (Self-Governing)

### First Marriage: Instant, Free, No Approval
1. Collector owns a Turbomindz NFT
2. Collector uploads 6+ photos of physical artwork + details
3. Smart contract records the marriage immediately on Polygon
4. NFT metadata updates: "Married to [Art Title]"
5. Card flip UI activates showing NFT front / physical art back
6. Marriage appears in the Provenance Museum

### Re-Marriage (Changing Artwork): Community Governed
1. Collector submits a "Change Marriage" request
2. Request posted in #marriage-requests Discord channel
3. @Elder and @Scribe role holders vote (3 of 5 approve)
4. If approved: $20 MATIC fee, old art unlinked, new art linked
5. If rejected: collector keeps current marriage

### Community Flagging
- Any member can flag a suspicious marriage (stock photos, AI fakes)
- 3 unique flags trigger automatic community review
- If confirmed fraudulent: marriage revoked, collector warned
- Repeat offenders: @Collector role revoked

### Why Self-Governing Works
- Zero bottleneck on the founder — marriages happen 24/7
- Community has ownership over quality standards
- Public photos in Provenance Museum = natural transparency
- Bad actors are caught by the community, not a single reviewer
- Scales infinitely — 10 marriages or 10,000, same system
