**Description**:
Have you ever stood inside a thought? That's what it felt like when Cosmo first entered the Crystalline Dreamscape — a universe where every surface remembers the light that touched it, and every crystal hums a frequency that sounds suspiciously like poetry.

"If you want to build a ship," Saint-Exupéry once wrote, "teach them to long for the endless immensity of the sea." Nova traced three fingers across the crystal wall and smiled. "Same pattern," she murmured. "Every great builder starts with longing, not lumber."

Stella's constellation freckles caught the prismatic light, scattering tiny stars across her cheeks like the cavern was decorating her. "But how do people actually USE longing?" she asked, already sketching community blueprints in her mind.

Five secrets hide in these crystals. A rose. A fox. A notebook that shouldn't be there yet. Look closely — beauty reveals itself to those who search.
